Northeast Grocery Systems Warehouse Management Systems

Exceed application - Receiving is the process of bringing products purchased from a vendor into a warehouse. Products are usually stored for some length of time and then shipped to the store. When a delivery truck arrives at the warehouse, the warehouse personnel must be equipped and prepared to transfer the product from the truck to the appropriate storage location.This requires scheduling of receipts though the appointments maintenance system. The host systems transfers information to the EXE to facilitate the receipt of the product.The EXE represents the truck as an appointment. The appointment is basically a list of POs to be received and the expected time of truck arrival to the WHSE. This allows the personnel to plan receiving to avoid congestion etc. and move the products to appropriate storage locations. As the WHSE knows in advance what is in the truck, he/she can create all receiving documents including pallet labels of what is being received on a particular PO, before the truck arrives. Putaway is a process of taking the pallets from the receving doors to the appropriate storage location within the warehouse, thus, making the pallet part of the Inventory. Verification is the process of double-checking the receivers and making sure that they didn’t miscount the received quantity, and to close the PO and complete the appointment
